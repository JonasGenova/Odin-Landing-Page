Catch Your Dreams Before They Fade

NapBook is your cozy online dream journal — a safe space to capture, explore, and rediscover the hidden stories your mind writes each night.

FEATURES

Dream Capture

Write down last night’s adventure before it fades into the morning mist. Your personal dream journal, safe and searchable.

Lucid Lens

An AI “Dream Oracle” that highlights symbols, connects patterns, and gives playful interpretations of what your subconscious might be saying.

Astral Replay

Step back into your dream world with an interactive playback mode. Navigate your dream journal like a map of constellations

Noctis Graphs

Visualize your dream world: see recurring themes, moods, and hidden storylines through elegant charts and timelines.

Lunar Threads

Share your wildest dreams anonymously with the community — or browse others’ surreal stories for inspiration and laughs.
